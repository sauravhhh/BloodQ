Blood Components & Types

Understanding the vital fluid that sustains life

Blood Components

Blood is a specialized body fluid that delivers necessary substances to the body's cells and transports waste products away from those same cells. It consists of several components:

Red Blood Cells

Carry oxygen from lungs to tissues

White Blood Cells

Defend against infections

Platelets

Help blood clot and stop bleeding

Plasma

Liquid portion that carries cells

Blood Types

Human blood is grouped into four types: A, B, AB, and O. Each blood type is also classified by its Rh factor as either positive (+) or negative (-).

Did you know? Type O negative blood is considered the "universal donor" because it can be given to people of any blood type. Type AB positive is the "universal recipient" because people with this type can receive any blood type.
